## Who We Are

TexSet is an open-source initiative built around a simple idea: writing in LaTeX should feel modern, smooth, and free. We're a small community of developers, writers, and LaTeX enthusiasts who believe the tools we use every day deserve thoughtful design and full transparency.

This organization is the home for everything we build — from the editor itself to the infrastructure that lets you run it anywhere.

## What We're Building

We're creating a LaTeX editing experience that combines a clean, modern interface with the power and flexibility of self-hosting. Something you can spin up on your own machine or server, make your own, and trust completely because it's open for anyone to inspect.

Our focus is on getting the fundamentals right first: a fast and pleasant editor, reliable compilation, and a setup process that doesn't get in your way.
